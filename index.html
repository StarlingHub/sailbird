How It Works

Three Steps to Activate Safe Insulin Cooling

From tap water to travel-ready in just 10 minutes. No tools, no training, no cold chain required.

1

Soak in Tap Water

Submerge the inner cooling pouch in ordinary tap water for 10 minutes.

2

Place Your Insulin

Leave the inner cooling pouch on a towel without touching it to remove excess water, and place your insulin pens, vials, or temperature-sensitive medication snugly inside the activated cooling pouch.

3

Go Anywhere, Safely

Place it into the outer cover and go. FROiD maintains 18–30°C for 50+ hours — on flights, road trips, at work, in rural areas, or remote healthcare missions.
